Research Intern, Usable Privacy and Security
Indian Institute of Technology Gandhinagar  ·  Survey and qualitative interview methodology

My research interests are in usable privacy and security: understanding how people perceive, reason about, and respond to the privacy and security properties of systems they use. Security technologies succeed or fail at the human level, yet the cognitive, behavioral, and contextual factors shaping user engagement with protective systems remain poorly understood.

I hold a secondary interest in human-AI interaction, particularly in how AI systems shape human judgment and trust in safety-critical and security-relevant contexts.
